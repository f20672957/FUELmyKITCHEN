About us!

Greetings and welcome to FUELmyKITCHEN™!

My name is Wadih and I am a computer science student, majoring at AOU, presenting to you my final year project. My journey at AOU has been full of valuable experience shaping me into the programmer/computer-whizz I am today. Even though this would be the last step I take towards my major at AOU and my ultimate goal so soon to be fulfilled, I find myself enormously grateful for all the memories I made along this journey. All the way since my first year at AOU, anxiously walking onto my first class, to when I turn in the last line of code for my project. The journey has been incredibly sweet. With all the programming and research skills I've gained while making it, FUELmyKITCHEN™ will serve as the portal that will initiate my career in the computer industry.

In brief, FUELmyKITCHEN™ is a prototype web application designed to address problems that could arise in a person's kitchen. It consists of two main pages, the landing page, featuring an option to subscribe to weekly/monthly newsletters, and the recipes page. The recipes page, the core of FUELmyKITCHEN™, features a search engine nicknamed "FINDmyRECIPE" and an index containing several recipes, each of which has a complete page full of detailed information related to the recipe including instructions, ingredients, a FAQ section, and much more!

I sincerely hope you have a pleasant visit, and I eagerly await your feedback!
~ WADIH
